## How To Study Without Getting Lost

Use this order:

1. Read the weekly file first.
2. Try the practice exercises.
3. If stuck, read one resource section.
4. Come back to the exercise.
5. Write one short explanation in your own words.

Stop after 60 minutes unless your teacher asks for more. The goal is steady progress, not exhaustion.
